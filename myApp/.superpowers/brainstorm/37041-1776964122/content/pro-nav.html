Pro Mode — Navigation Structure

6 sections to fit into a mobile nav. Which pattern feels right?

A

4-Tab Bar (recommended)

Home · Slots · Services · Account
History lives inside Home (past bookings section). Subscription lives inside Account. Mirrors customer tab count — feels familiar.

B

3-Tab Bar + "More" drawer

Home · Slots · More ···
More → History, Services, Settings, Subscription. Keeps tab bar minimal but buries important sections behind a tap.

C

5-Tab Bar

Home · Slots · History · Services · Account
Everything visible. Settings + Subscription live inside Account. More taps in the bar — can feel crowded on small screens.

Context

Customer tab bar has 3 tabs: Home · Bookings · Account. The Pro tab bar will replace these entirely when role = pro.
